Dresden

Dresden is a charming city in eastern Germany, known for its rich history and stunning Baroque architecture. Situated on the banks of the River Elbe, it stands out for its impressive urban landscape and cultural heritage.

Some notable places to visit in Dresden include:

  1. The Semper Opera House
  2. The Church of Our Lady (Frauenkirche)
  3. The Zwinger Palace
  4. The Green Vault Museum
  5. The Albertinum Museum
